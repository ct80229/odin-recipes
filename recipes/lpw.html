Lemon Pepper Wet

Description

Cook Out Wings from Atlanta

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings, separated into pieces

  • 1 Tbsp lemon-pepper seasoning (see below to make seasoning from scratch)

  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce (for JR Crickets version)

  • 1/4 cup clarified butter (for American Deli version)

  • 2 quarts vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat frying oil to 350Â°F.

  2. Fry wings for 7-10 minutes, until golden brown and crisp.

  3. Toss with lemon pepper seasoning, followed by buffalo sauce.
